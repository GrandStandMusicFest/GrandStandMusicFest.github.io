GrandStand Music Festival is a nonprofit organization that merges the arts with social activism into one-of-a-kind events that take a stand against sex trafficking in the state of Michigan.

GrandStand Music Festival aims to enrich Michigan communities through high quality musical performances, and to use these concerts to raise funding and awareness to help combat sex trafficking in Michigan

All proceeds will be donated to House of Promise. For more information about their organization, click here.
